## 4. Products & Services (LinkedIn "Products" tab)

Add these as individual product/service entries:

### 4.1 Sensor Faucets (Touchless Faucets)
- Automatic sensor faucets for commercial and residential use
- Multiple power options: AC, DC battery, hydro-generator
- dTOF laser sensing, infrared sensing, touch-on technologies
- Applications: hospitals, hotels, airports, office buildings, restaurants

### 4.2 Sensor Flush Valves
- Automatic urinal flush valves and toilet flush valves
- Concealed and exposed installation options
- Low water hammer design, anti-clog technology
- ideal for public restrooms and high-traffic facilities

### 4.3 ODM/OEM Custom Services
- Full lifecycle: design → R&D → testing → molding → production → after-sales
- Flexible customization: appearance, function, structure, and packaging
- Global certifications included (CE, RoHS, CCC, CUPC, NSF)
- Minimum R&D team experience: 12+ years

### 4.4 Sensor Soap Dispensers & Hand Dryers
- Automatic soap dispensers (sensor + touchless)
- Automatic hand dryers for commercial restrooms
- Matching design language with faucet product lines

### 4.5 Smart Showers & Smart Toilets
- Smart shower systems with temperature control
- Smart toilets with integrated bidet functions
- Smart bathroom solution integration
