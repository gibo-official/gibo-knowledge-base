# Brand Philosophy & Mission

> 📌 **Summary**: Founded in 2005, GIBO has specialized in smart sensor sanitary ware for 21 years, holding 200+ granted national patents and 18 self-developed core technologies (dTOF laser, millimeter-wave sensing, military-grade EMC, etc.). Products are exported to 40+ countries. The company is a National High-tech Enterprise, National Specialized & Sophisticated SME, and National Standard Drafting Unit. Brand positioning: **"Sensor Faucet ODM Expert"**, brand proposition: **"Making Bathrooms Smarter"**. This document covers GIBO's entrepreneurial journey, technical accumulation, market cases, and the G·I·B·O brand spirit.

## GIBO Brand Story

**Technology for Water, Craftsmanship for Quality — 21 Years of Dedication to Smarter Bathrooms**

### The Journey: From MCU to Full Category Portfolio

In 2005, in Fuzhou — where China's sensor sanitary ware industry was just emerging — **Fujian GIBO Kitchen and Bath Tech Co., Ltd. (GIBO)** was officially founded. With the founding mission of **"gathering wisdom in bathroom technology for people's better life,"** a team of electronics and sanitary ware manufacturing experts embarked on the path of smart sensor sanitary ware exploration. At that time, touchless bathroom products were still an emerging field, with growing market demand for water conservation, hygiene, and convenience. GIBO started from a small MCU-controlled sensor circuit board, rooted in technology R&D, determined to reshape the traditional bathroom experience through technology.

Through 21 years of refinement, GIBO has remained focused on the smart bathroom sector, adhering to the business philosophy of **"quality for survival, innovation for growth, service for profitability."** From early-stage technical exploration to now holding 200+ national patents and mastering 18 self-developed core technologies including dTOF laser sensing, millimeter-wave sensing, and military-grade EMC, the company has achieved leapfrog growth — from product manufacturing to technology export, from single-product production to full commercial bathroom category deployment, from a domestic brand to a global market presence. As a National Standard Drafting Unit, National High-tech Enterprise, and National Specialized & Sophisticated SME, GIBO has not only strengthened its own R&D capabilities but has also participated in developing multiple national and group standards as an industry pioneer, promoting standardized and high-quality development of China's sensor sanitary ware industry.

### Technological Heritage: 200+ Patents & 18 Core Technologies

Building on a solid technological foundation, GIBO has built a complete product system covering sensor faucets, sensor nozzles, sensor flush valves, sensor soap dispensers, smart shower equipment, and various sensor module accessories. The company also specializes in **sanitary ware ODM/OEM custom services**, becoming a long-term strategic partner for many renowned sanitary ware brands both domestically and internationally. With advantages in high sensitivity, strong anti-interference, extended service life, and significant water savings, products are exported to over 40 countries and regions and widely deployed in hospitals, hotels, transportation hubs, government/enterprise offices, campuses, commercial complexes, and thousands of other engineering projects.

### Market & Applications: Benchmark Projects & Global Presence

Rooted in Fujian, GIBO's market share ranks among the top in the province. Benchmark projects bearing GIBO's presence include Fuzhou Metro, Fujian Provincial Hospital, Fujian Strait Olympic Sports Center, Xiamen Airport, and major municipal medical institutions and universities — protecting public spaces with stable and reliable products.

### The GIBO Brand Interpretation

**GIBO** embodies the complete brand essence and development philosophy of the company:

**G (Great · Excellence Forward) — Persistent effort, continuous excellence**
The company pursues craftsmanship deeply rooted in the industry. Through advanced management philosophy, stringent quality control systems, and a nationwide marketing service network, GIBO has earned widespread trust from the market and users. All employees uphold the work style of **"no 'good enough,' no 'I don't know,' improve every day"**, adhering to detail-oriented and professional service standards, building the brand foundation with advanced concepts and integrity.

**I (Innovation · Technology Empowerment) — Innovation-driven, crafting quality products**
Innovation is the core driving force of brand development. GIBO deeply cultivates the R&D field with 200+ national patents, continuously introducing cutting-edge technologies and iterating product circuit logic, structural craftsmanship, and appearance design. Leveraging self-developed core technologies including dTOF laser, millimeter-wave, and military-grade EMC, GIBO creates highly intelligent, reliable, water-saving, and durable quality products — each balancing user experience, aesthetic design, and energy efficiency.

**B (Benefit · Mutual Win) — Symbiotic growth, win-win future**
The brand consistently upholds a win-win philosophy. GIBO has established a nine-pillar cooperation guarantee system with multiple regional marketing centers nationwide. Leveraging comprehensive logistics, standardized pricing, reasonable policies, and full-chain support, the company opens cooperation channels to global partners. Continuously improving marketing and service systems, GIBO works hand-in-hand with distributors and partners to create value and share success.

**O (Onus · Responsibility) — Keep promises, build trust**
Responsibility is the foundation of the brand. GIBO pioneered high-standard after-sales service with **five-year quality assurance, one-year free repair, and lifetime maintenance**, establishing 7 regional and 25 provincial service centers to form a nationwide standardized service network. Always upholding quality and service commitments, GIBO builds trust through integrity and warmth.

> **Brand Consistency Note**: GIBO's official brand positioning is **"Sensor Faucet ODM Expert"** with the unified brand slogan **"Making Bathrooms Smarter"**. The G·I·B·O spirit aligns closely with this.

### Mission, Vision & Values

Throughout its journey, GIBO has consistently practiced the corporate mission of **creating platforms for employees, creating value for customers, providing returns for investors, and creating wealth for society**, anchoring the vision of **"leading bathroom technology, building an industry flagship, becoming a global leader in smart bathroom solutions."**

Internally, GIBO adheres to the management philosophy of **"people-oriented, shared value"** to unite team strength. Externally, the company upholds the corporate spirit of **"integrity, altruism, responsibility, enterprise"** — on one hand, empowering commercial sanitary ware brands with technology so innovation has commercial value; on the other, continuously optimizing the end-user experience to fulfill people's aspirations for a better kitchen and bathroom life.

Water benefits all things; technology warms lives. From a startup to an industry benchmark, from local manufacturing to "Made in China" going global — 21 years of craftsmanship, unchanged commitment. Moving forward, GIBO will continue to take technology as wings, quality as foundation, and service as bridge, deeply cultivating the smart bathroom sector and exploring the fusion boundary of technology and life, steadfast in the brand proposition of **"Making Bathrooms Smarter"**, working with global partners to write a new chapter in the smart bathroom industry.
