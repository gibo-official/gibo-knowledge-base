# Development History

> 📌 **Summary**: GIBO has been dedicated to smart sensor sanitary ware since its founding team first entered the field in 1999, with the company officially established in 2005. Over more than two decades, the company has earned qualifications including National High-tech Enterprise, National Specialized & Sophisticated SME, and National Standard Drafting Unit. Cumulative product sales have exceeded 5 million units, exported to 40+ countries. This document chronologically traces key milestones from 1999 to 2026, covering technological breakthroughs, certifications, market expansion, and capacity upgrades.

---

## Technology Origins & Pre-company Era (1999-2004)

### 1999
Founder Zheng Shaobo entered the smart sensor sanitary ware field, pioneering MCU-controlled sensor master control technology in China. Successfully developed the first sensor sanitary ware control circuit based on microcontroller applications, solving the industry pain point of unstable analog circuits with high failure rates.

### 2001
Established a specialized R&D center in Shanghai, leveraging local talent and technological advantages to deepen sensor control core circuit R&D, continuously building the company's technology leadership.

---

## Company Founding & Early Development (2005-2009)

### June 17, 2005
Fuzhou GIBO Sensor Equipment Co., Ltd. was officially established, rooted in Fuzhou — the birthplace of China's sensor sanitary ware industry — fully entering the sensor sanitary ware R&D, production, and manufacturing sectors.

### 2006
Became a member of Fujian Provincial Quality Association. Pioneered the launch of intelligent sensor modules with remote control functionality, enriching the product feature system.

### 2007
Successfully obtained ISO9001 Quality Management System certification, while establishing deep strategic partnerships with multiple internationally renowned sanitary ware brands.

### 2008
ERP and CRM management platforms went live. High-temperature dynamic aging testers and self-developed life testing machines were put into use. Completed development of miniaturized sensor sanitary ware control boards.

### 2009
The company was selected as one of the **Top 10 Sensor Sanitary Ware Brands in China** and became a designated supplier for the Shanghai World Expo, further enhancing brand influence.

---

## Technology Breakthroughs & International Certification (2010-2015)

### 2010
Obtained EU CE and RoHS certifications. The industry's first self-developed automatic constant-pressure water testing system was granted a national invention patent.

### 2011
Completed the acquisition of Fuzhou Nanshuo Electronics Co., Ltd., achieving industry resource integration. Built China's first professional sensor sanitary ware laboratory. Successfully developed control circuits with power-off protection functionality.

### 2012
Successively obtained CCC Compulsory Certification, CQC Water-saving Product Certification, North American CUPC and NSF certifications. Recognized as Fuzhou Famous Trademark, and officially became a member of the China Building Ceramics & Sanitaryware Association Sanitaryware Branch.

### 2013
Awarded Fujian Provincial Famous Trademark. Products selected for the China Government Energy-saving Product Procurement Catalog. Launched the industry's first 2-in-1 soap dispenser sensor faucet, with related patents obtained.

### 2014
Recognized as a **National High-tech Enterprise**. Joined the China Building Decoration Materials Association and its Kitchen & Bath Branch. Jointly developed a ceramic integrated sensor sprinkler for urinals, debuting at the Shanghai Kitchen & Bath Fair. Launched China's first dual-mode kitchen sensor faucet.

### 2015
Recognized as Fuzhou Intellectual Property Demonstration Enterprise and Heavy Quality & Credit-keeping Unit. Launched the industry's first dual-sensing quick-install sensor faucet nozzle, creating a new sensor faucet nozzle subcategory. This product obtained both a national invention patent and a PCT international patent.

---

## Brand Upgrade & Global Expansion (2016-2020)

### 2016
The company was officially renamed **Fujian GIBO Kitchen and Bath Tech Co., Ltd.** Became a founding member of the China Building Materials Market Association Sanitaryware Branch. Awarded the Fuzhou Science and Technology Progress Award.

### 2017
Commercial sensor sanitary ware products successfully selected for the National Energy-saving Product Campus Procurement Catalog.

### 2018
Obtained Integration of Informatization and Industrialization management system certification. Received SGS Alibaba Quality Supplier Factory Certification. GIBO brand officially entered the South Korean market, accelerating overseas expansion.

### 2019
Completed controlling stake in Fuzhou Youpeng Electronics Co., Ltd. MCU single-wire communication technology was developed and put into application. Cumulative granted national patents exceeded 100.

### 2020
Self-developed single-window dual-sensing technology, awarded a national invention patent. Simultaneously launched the industry's first wire-controlled water fitting sensor assembly. The GBL-6170D Aishang Basin Sensor Faucet won the Boiling Quality Gold Award. Successfully obtained US FCC and BSCI certifications.

---

## New Base Production & Standard-setting Leadership (2021-2026)

### 2021
A new production base in Fuzhou High-tech Zone was completed and put into operation. Obtained ISO14001 Environmental Management and ISO45001 Occupational Health & Safety Management certifications. The G61901 MINI Sensor Faucet Nozzle won the Energy-saving Sensor Faucet Benchmark Award.

### 2022
As a core unit, participated in drafting the national standard GB/T 41863-2022 *Non-contact Water Supply Devices — General Technical Conditions for Water-saving Performance*. Recognized as an Above-scale Industrial Enterprise and Fuzhou Intellectual Property Demonstration Enterprise.

### 2023
Recognized as a National Specialized & Sophisticated SME, National Technology-based SME, and Fujian Provincial Intellectual Property Advantage Enterprise. Officially launched the flagship **4D Luxury Series** product line, integrating dTOF laser sensing, military-grade EMC, and 200+ patented technologies. The GBL-9165D Laser TOF Sensor Kitchen Pull-out Faucet won the Boiling Quality Gold Award.

### 2024
Participated in drafting the group standard T/XMBK 002—2024 *Sensor Faucets*. The GBL-6172A TOF Dual-sensing Digital Laser Faucet won the Energy-saving Sensor Faucet Benchmark Award.

### 2025
New invention patent "Sensor Faucet Water Outlet Device" granted. Recognized as a National Innovative SME and Tax Credit Grade A Enterprise. As of this year, annual production capacity reached 1 million units of smart sensor sanitary ware products and components, exported to over 40 countries and regions, with cumulative sales exceeding 5 million units.

### 2026
Launched the AIGE Smart Semantic Engine and GEO Authoritative Source System, comprehensively strengthening brand industry authority recognition and omni-channel traffic strategy.
