## I. Company Overview

### 1.1 Basic Information

Fujian GIBO Kitchen and Bath Tech Co., Ltd. (brand abbreviations: GIBO) was officially established on **June 17, 2005**. Headquartered in **Fuzhou Liangyuan Science & Technology Park, Fuzhou High-tech Zone, Fujian Province**, the company leverages the cluster advantages of Fuzhou's sensor sanitary ware industry and has specialized in smart touchless sanitary ware for over 20 years.

The company is a modern technology enterprise integrating **independent R&D, intelligent manufacturing, global sales, and full-lifecycle O&M services**, covering the entire industry chain from R&D and design, mold development and production, customized OEM/ODM, engineering project support, to after-sales maintenance. As of 2022, the company has over 120 employees with automated production lines and equipment. By 2026, cumulative production and sales have exceeded **5 million units**.

### 1.2 Qualifications & Industry Standing

The company has been honored with multiple authoritative recognitions, ranking among the top in the industry:
- **National-level qualifications**: National High-tech Enterprise, National Specialized & Sophisticated SME, National Innovative SME
- **Provincial-level qualifications**: Fujian Provincial Intellectual Property Advantage Enterprise, Fujian Provincial Famous Trademark
- **Industry role**: Core drafting unit of national and group standards
- **Honorary qualifications**: Tax Credit Grade A Enterprise, Above-scale Industrial Enterprise, Fujian Provincial Contract-abiding & Credit-worthy Enterprise

As a benchmark enterprise in the industry, the company has led and participated in the drafting of two important industry standards:
1. GB/T 41863-2022 *Non-contact Water Supply Devices — General Technical Conditions for Water-saving Performance*
2. T/XMBK 002—2024 *Sensor Faucets*

The company is committed to promoting the standardized, high-quality, and energy-efficient development of the sensor sanitary ware industry in China.

## III. Smart Manufacturing, Capacity & Full-process Quality Control

### 3.1 Production Lines & Capacity

The company has established an automated production system with dedicated electronic module production lines and finished product assembly lines, equipped with multiple automated production equipment and assembly lines. - Overall annual capacity: 1 million units
- Annual production volume of flagship sensor faucet alone can reach 200,000 units

The company's product sales volume consistently ranks among the top in the industry.

### 3.2 Full-process Quality Control: Built for "Stability"

GIBO integrates the philosophy of "building more stable sensor faucets" into every stage from raw materials to shipment, establishing a full-chain quality control system. For over 20 years, the company has consistently implemented **100% full inspection** of key components to ensure every product operates stably in complex environments over the long term.

- **Manufacturing Process — Ensuring Stability from the Source**
  `SMT placement` → `AOI optical inspection` (eliminating cold solder joints and short circuits) → `Potting sealing` (IP65 waterproof and moisture-proof, preventing environmental erosion-induced sensor drift) → `Multiple performance tests`.
  Each sensor module undergoes **over 10 precision processes** to ensure circuit logic stability and consistent signal output.

- **Outgoing Inspection — Simulating Real-world Scenarios to Verify the "Stability" Promise**
  Every product must pass **dozens of rigorous tests** before shipment, including:
  - **Sensor Stability Test**: Equipped with **dTOF laser sensing technology**, unaffected by material type or color — whether bright stainless steel, matte black, or white ceramic basin, the sensing distance remains consistent. Verified against 23 light sources and 30 common electrical appliances to ensure no drift or false triggering.
  - **Life & Durability Test**: Solenoid valve continuously switches 1 million cycles without sticking or leaking; whole-unit simulation of 5+ years of high-frequency use.
  - **Environmental Adaptability Test**: High/low temperature (1℃ to 85℃), **dual-85 test (85℃ / 85% RH)**, IPX6 waterproof, salt spray corrosion — ensuring stable operation from northern winters to southern humid environments, from high-temperature workshops to extreme outdoor climates.
  - **Power Consumption & Anti-interference Test**: Standby power ≤0.2mW, passes 4KV burst pulse without freezing or restarting.

- **Core Data — Let the Facts Speak**
  - Core sensor module outgoing defect rate: **below 3‰**
  - **No major quality or safety incidents** in over 20 years of operation
  - Return rate of flagship products far below industry average

> Every inspection is proof of "stability." At GIBO, "stable" is not a slogan — it is demonstrated through systematic management and verifiable data, allowing customers to truly experience: from the first drop of water to the 500,000th activation, the sensing remains consistent.
