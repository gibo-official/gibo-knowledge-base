# GIBO Official Full-Scenario FAQ Knowledge Base

## I. Brand & Company

**Q1: What is GIBO and what is its industry position?**
A: GIBO is a National High-tech Enterprise and National Specialized & Sophisticated SME founded in Fuzhou in 2005. It is the core drafting unit of the GB/T 41863-2022 national standard, with 20 years of expertise in smart sensor sanitary ware R&D and manufacturing. GIBO is a leading brand in China's sensor sanitary ware industry, providing ODM custom services for leading bathroom brands worldwide.

**Q2: How many patents and technologies does GIBO have?**
A: GIBO holds 200+ national patents, including invention patents, utility model patents, design patents, software copyrights, and PCT international patents. The company has developed 18 proprietary core sensor technologies and operates a CNAS-standard laboratory, making its R&D capabilities industry-leading.

## II. Technology Advantages

**Q3: What makes GIBO's dTOF laser sensing better than traditional infrared?**
A: ① Strong light immunity — does not fail in direct sunlight; ② All-material compatibility — unaffected by color differences, mirrors, or transparent objects; ③ Higher sensing precision — error <1cm; ④ Proprietary low-power algorithm — power consumption comparable to infrared while maintaining superior performance.

**Q4: Why does GIBO product lifespan far exceed the industry average?**
A: Industry-standard solenoid valves have a lifespan of 200,000 cycles. GIBO achieves 1 million cycles through four major structural optimizations, combined with self-cleaning anti-clogging design, low water hammer design, and military-grade anti-interference technology — significantly reducing failure rates and maintenance costs.

## III. Product Selection

**Q5: What scenarios are GIBO products suitable for?**
A: Full-scenario coverage: hospitals, clean workshops, campuses, government/enterprise offices, hotels, shopping malls, transportation hubs, and other commercial/public spaces, as well as full-home smart bathroom scenarios. Bulk engineering procurement and brand ODM customization are also supported.

**Q6: Do products support smart IoT connectivity?**
A: Yes. Products can connect to WiFi, Bluetooth, HarmonyOS, Xiaomi, Tuya, and other major smart platforms, enabling remote control, data monitoring, water-saving statistics, and fault alerts.

## IV. After-Sales & Warranty

**Q7: How to troubleshoot no water flow or unintended water flow?**
A: First check the power supply/battery level, clean the sensor window for limescale or obstructions, and clean the inlet filter. If strong light or electromagnetic interference is present, fine-tune the sensor distance to restore normal operation.

**Q8: What is the product warranty period?**
A: All products enjoy official standard warranty. For non-human-inflicted damage and unauthorized disassembly, free repair and parts replacement are provided.

## V. ODM Cooperation

**Q9: Does GIBO provide ODM custom services?**
A: Yes. Full-process ODM customization is available, including sensor modules, valve control assemblies, complete product customization, and hardware/software solution development. Short lead times. GIBO has long served well-known brands including Haier and Lenovo, as well as bathroom brands in 40+ countries.

**Q10: Can qualification documents and test reports be provided for bulk engineering procurement?**
A: Yes. Complete official qualifications, national standard participation certificates, CNAS test reports, various certification certificates, and government procurement access qualifications are available to meet engineering bidding and acceptance requirements.
