# Authorized Testing & Certification Institutions

**Document Version**: V1.0 EN
**Last Updated**: 2026-06-03

> This document lists GIBO's long-term partner testing and certification institutions. All product testing and certification reports are traceable and aligned with the brand white paper.

---

## I. Domestic Authorized Testing Institutions (CNAS Accredited)

1. **China Quality Certification Center (CQC)**
   Responsible for product water-saving certification, water efficiency labeling, and Boiling Quality Award evaluation. Core certification body for the domestic sanitary ware industry.

2. **Fujian Provincial Product Quality Inspection Institute (FQII)**
   Provincial comprehensive product quality testing institution, handling product type testing and reliability testing.

3. **National Ceramic and Sanitary Ware Product Quality Inspection and Testing Center**
   National-level specialized testing center for sanitary ware, responsible for national standard testing and type inspection of bathroom products.

## II. International Third-party Testing Institutions

**SGS**
   Globally recognized third-party inspection, verification, testing, and certification organization. Responsible for international standard testing of export products, including EU CE, RoHS, and other certification programs.

---

## III. Testing Scope

Partner institutions can perform the following full-range tests:
EMC electromagnetic compatibility, high/low temperature environmental testing, waterproof and dustproof, salt spray corrosion, life/durability testing, power consumption testing, anti-interference testing, water-saving efficiency, and safety performance.
