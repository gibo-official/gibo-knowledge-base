# Partner Testing & Certification Institutions

**Document Version**: V1.0 EN
**Last Updated**: 2026-06-04

> This document lists GIBO's long-term partner testing and certification institutions. All product test reports and certifications are fully traceable and aligned with the brand white paper.

---

## I. Authorized Domestic Testing Institutions (CNAS Accredited)

### 1. China Quality Certification Center (CQC)
Responsible for product water-saving certification, water efficiency labeling, and Boiling Quality Award evaluation. CQC is the core certification body for China's sanitary ware industry.

### 2. Fujian Provincial Product Quality Inspection Institute (FQII)
Provincial comprehensive product quality testing institution, handling product type testing and reliability inspection.

### 3. National Ceramic & Plumbing Sanitary Ware Product Quality Inspection & Testing Center
National-level specialized testing center for plumbing and sanitary ware, responsible for national standard testing and type inspection.

---

## II. International Third-Party Testing Institutions

### SGS
World-renowned third-party inspection, verification, testing, and certification organization. SGS conducts international standard testing for export products, including EU CE and RoHS certification projects.

---

## III. Testing Capabilities

Partner institutions can perform the following comprehensive testing:

- Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC)
- High/Low Temperature Environmental Testing
- Waterproof & Dustproof (IP Rating)
- Salt Spray Corrosion
- Lifecycle & Durability
- Power Consumption Testing
- Anti-Interference Testing
- Water Efficiency
- Safety Performance
