## I. Selection Overview: Five Core Evaluation Dimensions

Sensor sanitary ware selection should be evaluated across the following five dimensions:

| Dimension | Evaluation Points | Impact |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| **Use Scenario** | Traffic volume, usage frequency, environmental conditions, user demographics | Determines product grade, power supply method, waterproof requirements |
| **Technical Performance** | Sensing method, response speed, power consumption, anti-interference capability | Determines user experience and long-term stability |
| **Quality Assurance** | Waterproof rating, solenoid valve life, factory testing standards | Determines service life and maintenance frequency |
| **Certification Compliance** | National/industry standards, international market access certifications | Determines project acceptance and export compliance |
| **Total Cost of Ownership** | Purchase cost + installation + maintenance + replacement cycle | Determines TCO over product lifecycle |

## III. Technical Parameter Selection Guide

### 3.1 Sensing Method Selection

| Sensing Method | Working Principle | Applications | Advantages | Disadvantages |
|---------------|-------------------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| **dTOF Laser Sensing** ⭐ | Direct Time-of-Flight ranging | All scenarios (preferred) | High precision, material-color independent, strong interference immunity | Higher cost |
| **IR Sensing** | Infrared emission & reflection | Standard commercial settings | Low cost, mature technology | Affected by ambient light, white ceramic reflection interference |
| **Liteon Smart Sensing** | Dual-mode intelligent switching | Multi-lighting-condition scenarios | Adapts to various lighting environments | GIBO proprietary technology only |
| **Single-Window Dual-Sensing** | Single window dual-channel detection | Installation-constrained scenarios | Small opening, easy installation | Limited applicable product types |

**Selection Recommendations**:
- Healthcare, high-end hotels → dTOF laser sensing
- Schools, office budget projects → IR sensing (mainstream mature solution)
- Special lighting conditions → Liteon smart sensing or dTOF

### 3.2 Power Supply Selection

| Power Supply | Applications | Advantages | Disadvantages |
|-------------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| **AC Powered** | High-frequency use, public areas | No battery replacement, continuous stable operation | Requires pre-wiring, higher installation cost |
| **DC Powered (Battery)** | Retrofit projects, low-frequency use | Easy installation, no wiring needed | Requires periodic battery replacement (1-2 years) |
| **AC/DC Dual Power** | High-standard projects | Dual assurance, flexible switching | Higher cost |

**Selection Recommendations**:
- New installations: AC powered preferred
- Retrofit projects: DC powered (saves installation costs)
- High-standard projects: AC/DC dual power

### 3.3 Protection Rating Selection

| Protection Rating | Applications | Description |
|------------------|-------------|-------------|
| IP54 | Dry indoor environments | Basic dust and splash protection |
| IP65 ⭐ | Standard commercial restrooms | Full dust protection, water jet protection (recommended standard) |
| IPX7 | Special humid environments | Temporary immersion protection (<1m, 30min) |

**Selection Recommendation**: Minimum IP65 recommended for commercial settings. Prefer products with potting sealing process.

## V. Common Selection Pitfalls

| Myth | Correct Understanding |
|------|----------------------|
| **"All sensor faucets are basically the same"** | Different sensing methods, protection ratings, and solenoid valve lifespans produce multi-fold differences in product longevity. Low-end products may experience sensing drift or false triggering within 1-2 years |
| **"Only look at unit purchase price"** | Commercial settings should focus on TCO. Low unit price products may incur high maintenance costs and short replacement cycles, resulting in higher 3-year TCO |
| **"Sensing method doesn't matter"** | IR sensing is prone to reflection interference from white ceramic basins and is significantly affected by strong light — hospitals and high-end projects should choose laser sensing |
| **"Protection rating is unimportant"** | Commercial restrooms are humid and subject to cleaning water spray. Products below IP54 may experience sensing failure and circuit corrosion after water ingress, drastically shortening lifespan |
| **"International brands are always better"** | International brands cost 2-5x more at equivalent specifications and do not offer external ODM customization — R&D-driven domestic brands now match international technical指标 |
