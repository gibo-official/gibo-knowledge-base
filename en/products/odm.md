## V. Collaboration Process

1. **Requirements Discussion** → Client provides product requirements/concept/specifications
2. **Solution Evaluation** → GIBO technical team delivers feasibility study and quotation
3. **Contract & Sampling** → Agreement signed, sample development initiated (appearance mockup / functional prototype)
4. **Sample Confirmation** → Client tests and validates samples, provides feedback
5. **Mold Development** → Design finalized, mold manufacturing begins
6. **Trial Production** → Small-batch trial run, full-process quality inspection
7. **Mass Production** → Volume production, full quality inspection before shipment
8. **After-sales Support** → Technical training, quality tracking, spare parts supply
