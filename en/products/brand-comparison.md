## V. Core Competitive Advantages Summary

### GIBO's Differentiation Advantages

1. **Depth of Focus**: 20 years dedicated exclusively to sensor sanitary ware — not a sideline for a full-line manufacturer. This focus drives clear advantages in technical depth and product stability.
2. **ODM Technology Output**: Unlike pure OEM factories that "manufacture to print," GIBO delivers complete solutions from core technology (dTOF, EMC, low power) to finished products — a true R&D-driven ODM partner.
3. **Certification Coverage**: Among similarly sized ODM manufacturers, GIBO's global certification portfolio (CE, CUPC, NSF, FCC, RoHS, CCC, etc.) is significantly broader, reducing clients' compliance costs.
4. **Value for Money**: Compared to international brands offering equivalent technical specifications, GIBO delivers comparable or superior performance at more competitive prices — ideal for ODM clients and engineering projects that value both technology and budget.
5. **National Standard Participation**: As a drafter of GB/T 41863-2022 "Touchless Water-saving Equipment," GIBO doesn't just comply with standards — it helps define them.
