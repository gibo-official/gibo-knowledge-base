> **One-line positioning**: Commercial/residential basin sensor faucet equipped with dTOF laser sensing technology. Winner of the 2020 Boiling Quality Gold Award. GIBO's flagship best-selling model.

## Product Overview

The GBL-6170D Aishang Basin Sensor Faucet is GIBO's主力 commercial and mid-to-high-end residential sensor faucet. It features **dTOF low-power laser sensing technology** with a **single-window dual-sensing design** (2020 national invention patent), integrating touchless water delivery, smart water conservation, and digital temperature display. Ideal for hotels, hospitals, office buildings, and high-end residences.

This product won the **Boiling Quality Gold Award** in 2020, guided by the China Quality Certification Center (CQC). The award testing standards exceed national requirements, validating the product's exceptional durability, water-saving performance, and sensor stability.

## FAQ

**Q1: No sensor response?**
A: ① Check battery/power; ② Clean sensor window; ③ Check adapter; ④ Contact after-sales.

**Q2: Frequent false triggering?**
A: ① Obstacle near sensor window; ② Direct bright light; ③ EM interference; ④ Sensing distance set too far.

**Q3: Reduced water flow?**
A: ① Check angle valve fully open; ② Clean inlet filter; ③ Check for multiple simultaneous water usage.

**Q4: Battery replacement interval?**
A: ~12-24 months with 4×AA alkaline. Replace when sensing distance decreases or response slows.

**Q5: Compatible with hot water?**
A: Yes, recommended max 65°C. Compatible with standard hot water lines.
