# G61901 MINI Sensor Nozzle

> **One-line positioning**: World's first quick-install sensor nozzle. National invention patent + PCT international patent (2015). Winner of the 2021 Energy-saving Sensor Faucet Benchmark Award. Created the "sensor nozzle" product category.

---

## Product Overview

The G61901 MINI Sensor Nozzle is GIBO's world-first **quick-install sensor nozzle**, launched in 2015. It transforms any standard faucet into a touchless sensor faucet without replacing the existing fixture. The product is protected by both a **Chinese national invention patent** and a **PCT international patent**, and won the **Energy-saving Sensor Faucet Benchmark Award** in 2021.

The **split quick-install structure** requires no faucet replacement, no electrical wiring — users can install it themselves in under 1 minute. Ultra-low standby power (≤0.2mW) means 4×AA batteries last 12-18 months.

This product served as the core technology prototype for the **Lenovo Lecoo Sensor Water-Saving S1** cross-industry collaboration, validating its market maturity.

## FAQ

**Q1: Which faucets are compatible?**
A: Most standard faucets with G1/2" or M22 internal thread.

**Q2: Reduced flow after installation?**
A: Clean the built-in filter. Check water pressure ≥0.05MPa.

**Q3: Battery life?**
A: ~12-18 months normal use. Shortening sensing distance indicates low battery.

**Q4: Can I add sensing to my existing faucet without replacing it?**
A: Yes. Replace only the aerator with G61901. Faucet appearance unchanged, function upgraded.
