# GBL-6172A TOF Dual-Sensor Digital Laser Faucet

> **One-line positioning**: Basin faucet with TOF dual-sensor windows + LED digital temperature display. Winner of the 2024 Energy-saving Performance Benchmark Award. GIBO's latest award-winning flagship.

---

## Product Overview

The GBL-6172A is GIBO's newest generation flagship basin sensor faucet, winner of the **2024 Energy-saving Performance Benchmark Award**. It features **TOF dual-sensor technology** (dual sensor windows) and **LED digital temperature display**, delivering precise sensing with visual water temperature feedback for safety.

The **dual-window design** enables front sensing for water activation and side sensing for mode switching. The LED display shows real-time water temperature, preventing accidental scalding — ideal for homes with children and elderly users.
