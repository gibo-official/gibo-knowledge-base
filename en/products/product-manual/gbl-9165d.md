# GBL-9165D Laser TOF Sensor Kitchen Pull-Out Faucet

> **One-line positioning**: Smart kitchen pull-out faucet with laser TOF sensing technology. Winner of the 2023 Boiling Quality Gold Award. Combines touchless sensing with dual-mode pull-out operation.

---

## Product Overview

The GBL-9165D is GIBO's flagship kitchen faucet, featuring **laser TOF sensing technology** combined with **pull-out spray head design** and **dual-mode water delivery**. It won the **Boiling Quality Gold Award** in 2023 — GIBO's second product to earn this honor.

---

## Core Technologies

| Technology | Description | Advantage |
|------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Laser TOF Sensing** | Time-of-Flight laser ranging | Works with any cookware material, unaffected by water temperature |
| **Pull-Out Design** | Retractable hose, dual-mode spray | Full sink coverage |
| **Dual Water Modes** | Continuous + Sensor-activated | Touchless for hygiene, continuous for filling |
| **Military-Grade EMC** | 4KV pulse test | Stable in kitchen multi-appliance environments |
| **IP65 Protection** | Full potting seal | Moisture resistant |
