## I. Sensor Technologies

### 1. Triangulation Ranging Sensing

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Principle** | Combines laser ranging and microwave sensing advantages, precisely calculates target distance and position using triangulation |
| **Features** | 30μA ultra-low power design, high precision, low false-positive rate |
| **Problems Solved** | High power consumption and false triggering of traditional infrared sensors in complex lighting |
| **Applications** | All sensor water output products, 100K+ units mass-produced |
| **Advantages** | Ultra-low power + high precision + mass production validation |

### 2. Low-power dTOF Laser Sensing

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Principle** | Direct Time-of-Flight laser detection, measures reflection time of laser pulses for precise distance and motion sensing |
| **Features** | Ultra-low standby power, high sensitivity |
| **Problems Solved** | False triggers and missed triggers; interference from ambient light |
| **Applications** | Premium sensor faucets, toilet flush valves |
| **Advantages** | Penetrates water mist and light stains, immune to ambient light interference |

### 3. Millimeter Wave Sensing

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Principle** | Uses millimeter wave microwave detection to sense human micro-movements |
| **Features** | Strong penetration, unaffected by light or obstructions |
| **Problems Solved** | IR and optical sensor failure in strong light, backlight, and obstructed scenarios |
| **Applications** | Urinal flush valves, harsh environment applications |
| **Advantages** | Immune to strong light, fog, and dust interference |
